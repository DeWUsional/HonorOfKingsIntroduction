Heroes and Their Lanes

In Honor of Kings, each hero is best suited for a particular lane or role on the map. Understanding where each hero excels can be the key to dominating your matches. Some heroes can play multiple different roles/lanes but one lane is more suited, like Musashi which can play Clash lane and Jungling (it is more recommended in Jungling)

Clash Lane

Heroes in the Clash Lane are often tanks or bruisers who can withstand a lot of damage while dealing out their own. They are crucial in skirmishes and team fights, providing front-line support for their team.

Mid Lane

Mid Lane heroes are typically mages or assassins who deal high burst damage. They control the center of the map, providing pressure and quick rotations to assist other lanes.

Farm Lane

Farm Lane is usually played by the marksmen who need time to gather gold and experience. They are the team's main source of damage later in the game, so they need protection during the early stages.

Jungling

Junglers move between lanes, securing objectives (red buffs, blue buffs and dragons) and ambushing enemies. They play a critical role in the early game by applying pressure across the map and setting up ganks.

Roaming

Roaming heroes support their team by moving between lanes and assisting where needed. They provide vision, crowd control, and can help turn the tide of battle by surprising the enemy.
